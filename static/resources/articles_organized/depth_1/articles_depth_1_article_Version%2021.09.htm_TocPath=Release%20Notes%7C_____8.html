Important stability update for the new DX and RX editions.

 

With firmware versions prior to 21.09, these models may experience a sporadic loss of transmit power, which could only be resolved by manual restart.

 

Therefore we recommend all customers of the DX and RX models to update to 21.09 to prevent this problem.
